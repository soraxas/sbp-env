Informedrrt-RRT*¶

The Informed-RRT* motion planning behaves similar to planners.rrtPlanner .RRTPlanner before a first solution is found. After an initial solution is found, this planner uses an ellipses heuristic to speed up convergence rate of the resulting solution.

The bulk of the implementation occurs in InformedRRTSampler. The Informed-RRT* is implemented by directly deriving the base motion planner as RRTPlanner, and registering its sampler as the InformedRRTSampler.
